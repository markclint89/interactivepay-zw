FAQ

Questions you probably have

Q Is it safe?

Yes. Interactive Pay is powered by ZB Bank, one of Zimbabwe's most trusted financial institutions. Your funds are held in trust and protected by bank-grade security standards.

Q How fast does money arrive?

Within seconds of a successful payment. You receive a WhatsApp notification and see it in your dashboard immediately — before your customer has even put their phone away.

Q What payment methods are accepted?

EcoCash, InnBucks, 1Money, O'mari, Visa, and Mastercard. Your customers pay with whatever wallet or card they already use — no new accounts, no app downloads.

Funds are held in trust by ZB Bank and transferred to your nominated account — which can be a bank account or mobile wallet, depending on what works best for your business.

Our local support team is ready to assist. We understand Zimbabwe's business landscape and will help you get set up and running without any confusion or overseas call centres.
